Mission

LC NOVA aims to create a new type of learning experience in the mid-pandemic world of 2020. What we mean by a new experience is, is that we are going through a new change that none of the other LCs before us ever had in a Virtual Environment. We'll teach the LCs after us get how to get a better grip on things that could help them in the future. Additionally, proving what the NOVA is capable of as an learning community.
