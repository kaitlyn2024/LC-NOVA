About

The LC NOVA is a branch of YearUp: a program facilitating hands-on training, real-world corporate access, a six-month internship, and unparalleled support. Students training specialties, career tracks, start dates, internship partners, and more.

Mission

LC Nova aims to provide a new experience in the world of 2020. What we mean by new experience is, is that we are going through a new change that none of the other LCs before us every had in a Virtual Environment. We aim to help newer LCs after us get a better grip on things that could help them in the future while also proving what we are capable of as an learning community.
